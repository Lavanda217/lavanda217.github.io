some information about me and JOIN MY TEAM

I am a young artist from New York who is full of ideas and inspiration! You can join my team and create art with me! I draw in different styles. From cubism to realism. I love academic drawing. It usually takes me about a year. I collaborate with several galleries in New York and in Colorado. In my business, my team is trying to be eco-friendly. We do not use plastic, bags and do not buy anything extra.
